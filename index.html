About Me

Born in 1982 in Canada, I've spent my career at the forefront of live streaming innovation. As Chief Innovation Officer at Stage TEN for the past 8 years, I lead the development of cutting-edge video technology that's transforming how people connect online.

My journey includes roles as CTO at Slyce (TSX-V), OcularAI, and HOVR.IT (which I co-founded in 2012). I'm passionate about democratizing video production and making professional-quality streaming accessible to everyone.

With a background from the University of Waterloo's Precision Controls Lab and medical research at the University of Guelph, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and innovative thinking to every project.
